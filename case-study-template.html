Designing for a Nation in Crisis: The Co-WIN Vaccine Booking Feature

How we tackled a high-panic, high-scale problem by prioritizing clarity and pre-empting user anxiety.

Company: Eka.Care (formerly Orbi Health) Role: Lead Product Designer Timeline: 2021

The Problem: Chaos and Anxiety

In 2021, India's COVID-19 vaccine rollout was chaotic. The government's Co-WIN portal was overwhelmed. Users faced a high-anxiety, low-information environment, desperately searching for slots for their families. Our challenge was to create a vastly superior, reassuring, and reliable booking experience within our consumer app.

My Role & Process

As the lead designer, I was responsible for the entire end-to-end user experience, from concept to launch. I worked directly with engineering to define specs, as we had no dedicated PM. My process was built around a single principle: **fight panic with clarity**.

  • **Problem Framing:** Identifying user anxiety points: "Are there slots?", "Did my booking go through?", "What do I need to bring?".
  • **Logic Mapping:** Charting out the complex states: Dose 1 vs Dose 2, vaccine type (Covishield/Covaxin), paid vs free, and the specific time gap rules between doses.
  • **Iterative Design:** Creating high-fidelity mockups in Figma that explicitly addressed every potential question on the screen itself.

Key Design Decisions & Challenges

1. The "Pre-emptive" Confirmation Screen

The booking confirmation wasn't just a success message. It was a contract of reassurance. I meticulously designed it to include explicit instructions to prevent anxiety: "Arrive 15 minutes prior," "Bring your Passport," and clearly stating "Dose 1 of Covishield" to leave no room for doubt.

2. The "Register for Alert" System & A Technical Reality Check

We designed a notification system for when slots opened in a user's district. However, this revealed a critical design-to-engineering gap: all alerts fired at midnight when the Co-WIN system updated, crashing our servers. It was a powerful lesson in understanding the full lifecycle of a feature, beyond the UI. We had to work with engineering to build a staggered notification delivery system.

The Impact

The feature was a massive success, helping millions of Indians secure vaccine slots during a critical time. It drove a huge surge in app downloads and established Eka.Care as a key player in the digital health space. Most importantly, it proved that thoughtful, empathetic design can make a tangible difference in people's lives, especially in moments of crisis.
