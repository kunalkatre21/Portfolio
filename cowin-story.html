The Setup: A High-Stakes Mandate Background: In 2021, India faced a monumental challenge: vaccinating its population amidst a devastating pandemic wave. The government released the Co-WIN APIs, creating a sudden, urgent opportunity. The business goal for Eka.Care was clear: leverage these APIs to build a superior, trustworthy public utility that could capture a massive, anxious user base and establish us as a key player in India's digital health ecosystem. User Goals (The Anxiety): The public sentiment was not one of simple "user needs"; it was a state of panic. My research into online forums and news reports revealed the core user anxieties: "Will I ever find a slot for my elderly parents?" "Is this third-party app even legitimate?" "How do I know this information is real and not a waste of my time?" My Role: As the sole designer on this initiative, I was tasked with creating a functional user experience under an extreme "one-day delivery mode" timeline. With no PM, my role expanded to de-risking the project by defining requirements, analyzing technical constraints, and creating a clear plan of action for the entire cross-functional team. Heading (h2): 2. Discovery: Finding Signal in the Noise Understanding the Gap: The existing official portal was functional but overwhelmed, creating a significant "trust gap." Users were desperate for an alternative that was not just faster, but clearer and more reliable. Competitive & API Analysis: I began by simultaneously analyzing the official API documentation and the few competitor apps that had launched. The APIs: The documentation revealed multiple ways to find appointments (by PIN, by district, etc.), but was unclear on error states and data freshness. Competitors: Early solutions were often simple mirrors of the government portal, inheriting its confusing user experience without adding significant value. Key Takeaway: The opportunity wasn't just to be faster; it was to be clearer. The winning platform would be the one that could best translate the API's raw data into a reassuring and anxiety-reducing experience.
